# SEO_STRATEGY.md

## 1. SEO Goal

The site must be discoverable as a Vietnamese wooden furniture showroom.

## 2. Requirements

- Product slug
- Product meta title
- Product meta description
- Open Graph image
- Sitemap
- Robots.txt
- Image alt text
- Server-rendered/static product pages where practical

## 3. Product Slug

Example:

```text
/san-pham/ban-tra-go-soi-tu-nhien
```

## 4. Metadata

Each product should have:

```text
seo_title
seo_description
primary_image
canonical_url
```

## 5. Structured Data

Can be added after product information is stable.

Possible schema:

```text
Product
LocalBusiness
Organization
```

## 6. Do Not

- Do not put all product data behind client-only rendering.
- Do not use meaningless slugs.
- Do not miss alt text for product images.
